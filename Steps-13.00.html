STEP 1: Create Year Slicer  and state Slicer.

  1. Double click on the  Slicer Icon On the visualization bar.
  2. Slicer visual will display on the report.
  3. Drag an drop the Year column from the calendar table.
  4. Do the same 1 to 3 process to create state slicer.


STEP 2:
  1. Click on the drop down  button from the title header.
  2. Select Drop down Option.
  3. Turn off the header:  Change the  items      front color : black         Front : Arial Black.
  4. Turn on the title .
  5. Type "Year".
  6. Turn on the border.      color light grey      radius:  10 .
  7. Fix the  Slicer Size:     Width :   222                                      Height :    72 .
  8. Do the same process to the State Slicer.
