7) Create the Cards. 14

To Create The Card is pretty much the same process of creating the slicers.

STEP 1: Create # Of Claims card  and Total Cost  card.

  1. Double click on the card Icon On the visualization bar.
  2. Card visual will display on the report
  3. Drag an drop the "# Of Claims" column from the TSA claim table.
  4. Do the same 1 to 3 process to create Total Cost Card.


STEP 2: Configure the Format.

  1. Click on the Format icon
  2. Fix the  Card  Size:     Weight :   222                    Height: 72
  3. Fix the Data Label :     Display Inits : Thousands            Text size :  30
  4. Turn Off Category .
  5. Turn on the title 
  6. Type "# of Claims"
  7. Turn on the border :       Color light grey      Radius:  10
  8. Select the "# of Claims" Column 
  9. Change the Format to:       Decimal Number.                             select the "," sign
  10. Do the same process For  the Total Cost card.
